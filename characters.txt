Jay Gatsby – A wealthy and mysterious man known for his lavish parties. 
He is deeply in love with Daisy Buchanan and made his fortune to win her back.

Nick Carraway – The novel’s narrator. 
A Yale graduate and Gatsby’s neighbor, he offers an outsider’s perspective on the events of the story.

Daisy Buchanan – Nick’s cousin and Gatsby’s former lover. 
She is beautiful but shallow, married to Tom Buchanan.

Tom Buchanan – Daisy’s wealthy, arrogant, and aggressive husband. 
He is unfaithful and holds racist and elitist views.
